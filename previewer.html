Learn how to upgrade your storage plan to enjoy uninterrupted service.

Upgrade your storage plan if you purchased directly from Adobe Home

Try the following steps to upgrade your storage plan if purchased directly from Adobe:

Do you have a Teams plan?

Sign in at https://account.adobe.com/plans.

Select Manage plan.

Select Add more storage in the Available actions section.

Choose your preferred storage quota from the drop-down.

Select Review upgrade.

Review all details about your upgrade, and select Confirm.

Upgrade your storage if you purchased with a redemption code

If you purchased your plan from a retailer or an Adobe reseller, you would have received a redemption code. Additional storage isn't currently available for plans purchased with a redemption code. However, this facility may become available soon. Meanwhile, to add storage, you can purchase a plan with higher storage capacity.

Result

Once successful, your Adobe storage plan is upgraded. You'll also receive a confirmation email from Adobe.
